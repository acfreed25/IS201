About Me

This is what I am about.

Since a young age I have had a desire to make a difference in the world. From a young age I have been told that "where much is given much is expected." I believe that to my core and am motivated to reach my full potential in all that I do.

I was born in Provo, Utah. Home of the rocky mountains and some of the greatest snow on earth. Spending time outdoors with my parents and 4 siblings was always a big part of my life. We enjoy doing everything from boating in the summers to skiing in the winter and most importantly... playing lacrosse all year round.


I went to Timpview High School which I loved. I had lots of great friends and enjoyed playing on the lacrosse team. After high school I served a 2 year mission for The Church Of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints in Cote d'Ivorie and Florida. Because of that exprience I speak french and haitian creole.


I am now studying at BYU while also participating as a member of the BYU Mens Lacrosse team. I am studying Economics and hoping to break into venture capital one day. I hope to have a fulfilling career to provide for my family and to go back in my community.
